Contract Terms & Conditions

Our contract agreements are designed to be straightforward and fair. Here are the key terms you should be aware of before signing:

  • Security deposit equal to one night's stay is required for all bookings
  • Cancellations made 48 hours before arrival receive a full refund
  • Late cancellations will be charged for one night's stay
  • Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM
  • Extended stays require a valid credit card on file
  • Monthly contracts require a 30-day notice for termination
